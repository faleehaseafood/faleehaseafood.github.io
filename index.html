about us

Faleeha Seafood Resto & Cafe

Faleeha Seafood Resto & Cafe was founded on June 1 2017, with the theme "TASTY SEAFOOD COMFY PLACE". We will always provide the best service for customers who come both from the delicious food, the comfort of the room, as well as the very pleasant atmosphere of the room.

We provide a variety of food and beverage dishes by prioritizing natural products, especially the sea. We also provide various other dishes to meet the demands of customers, such as: Western Food, Chinese Food, Italian Food, and Traditional Food.

Faleeha Seafood Resto & Cafe also facilitates venues for various events, such as: Birthdays, Seminars, Gatherings, Meetings/Presentations, Gatherings, Coffee Breaks, Outside Catering, Engagement.
